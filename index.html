// ABOUT US

Where Science
Meets Silicon

Founded in 2019, NovaMind is an independent research institute dedicated to understanding the computational underpinnings of human cognition. We combine advanced neuroimaging, computational modeling, and deep learning to answer the deepest questions about consciousness.

Our interdisciplinary team spans neuroscience, AI engineering, philosophy of mind, and clinical psychology — united by a singular mission: to decode what makes us human.
